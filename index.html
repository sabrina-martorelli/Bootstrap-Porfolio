About

Hola! I'm Sabrina, a software engineer who is now focusing on frontend development. Since I was a little girl, I was passionate of computing, following the steps of my dad with his Texas Instruments 99/4A. I studied computer science in Argentina and did postgraduate courses in Education Technology and Elearning. I did many roles as a php and web developer between 2000 and 2015. Since then I moved into elearning, first as a Moodle developer and then into a consulting role. In 2019 I had a lovely daughter, Madison, and continued my career into education technology. In 2022, I decided to go back to my passion, software engineering, enrolling into a front end development bootcamp. In this site you will find my work during the course and also previous but related work.
